HI, I’m Pathrick Rouise Nievera, the founder of the UptownKicks MNL shoes.
I started this business when my cousin invited me to buy thrifted
clothes, then after that, I cleaned the ones I bought and sold
each piece. I remember noticing that the items were worth seven hundred
pesos, then I resold them for one thousand four hundred pesos (1,400 pesos).
After that, I kept repeating the process until I saved enough money
to buy branded items. Then I thought, why not continue doing this
since it seemed easy to earn money by recycling items.
